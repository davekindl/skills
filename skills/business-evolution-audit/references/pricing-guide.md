# Pricing Guide

## Market Context

| Benchmark | Price Range |
|-----------|-----------|
| Traditional consulting firm digital audit | €5,000-15,000 |
| SEO agency technical audit | €500-2,000 |
| UX audit (freelance) | €1,000-3,000 |
| Competitor analysis (agency) | €2,000-5,000 |
| Full digital transformation roadmap | €10,000-50,000 |

Our positioning: **below agency, above automated tools, faster than both.**

## Production Cost Model (example)

| Output | Your Time | API Cost | Total Cost |
|--------|------------|----------|-----------|
| Teaser (automated) | 15 min review | ~€0.50 | ~€5 (your time at your hourly rate) |
| Snapshot Report | 1-2 hrs review + enrichment | ~€1 | ~€25-45 |
| Full Evolution Audit | 3-5 hrs review + enrichment | ~€2 | ~€65-105 |

**Margin at listed prices:**

| Tier | Price | Cost | Margin |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| Snapshot (€399 avg) | €399 | €35 | 91% |
| Full Audit (€1,999 avg) | €1,999 | €85 | 96% |
| Advisory (€749/mo avg) | €749/mo | ~€200/mo (4 hrs) | 73% |

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|---------|
| "Too expensive" | "The audit identified €[X] in annual opportunity. At €[price], it pays for itself if one recommendation generates just [fraction]% of that." |
| "We have our own team" | "Perfect -- the audit gives your team a prioritized roadmap instead of guessing. It's an accelerator, not a replacement." |
| "We already know our problems" | "Then the audit will confirm your priorities and add revenue projections your board can act on. If it finds nothing new, money-back guarantee applies." |
| "Can you do it for less?" | "The Snapshot Report at €[lower tier] covers the top 10 gaps with revenue estimates. It's the right starting point if budget is a constraint." |

## Discount Rules
- Never discount Full Audit below €999 (brand positioning)
- Bundle discount: Full Audit + 3-month Advisory = 15% off monthly rate
- Referral: if target refers another company, €200 credit on next engagement
- First audit for a new industry vertical: may price at Snapshot rate to build the industry template

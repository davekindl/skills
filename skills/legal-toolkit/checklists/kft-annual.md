# Kft. Annual Compliance Checklist

For a Hungarian limited liability company (Korlátolt Felelősségű Társaság). Run once per business year. Deadlines assume a calendar business year ( end 31 December); adjust if the company's business year differs. Source deadlines from your company registration details (`deadlines:` block) where present.

Business year: [YEAR]   |   Last completed: [DATE]

## 1. Annual Financial Report (Beszámoló)
- [ ] Prepare annual financial report (mérleg + eredménykimutatás + kiegészítő melléklet) — micro-entity (mikrogazdálkodói) form if eligible
- [ ] Member's meeting (taggyűlés) approves the report
- [ ] File (letétbe helyezés + közzététel) with the Company Information Service via the OBR / e-beszámoló portal
- **Deadline:** within 5 months of business-year end (calendar year → 31 May)
- [REVIEW] Confirm micro-entity eligibility thresholds with the accountant.

## 2. Corporate Income Tax (Társasági adó, TAO) or KIVA
- [ ] Determine regime in force this year (TAO 9% vs KIVA 10%)
- [ ] File annual return (TAO: form '29; KIVA: form 'KIVA)
- [ ] Reconcile quarterly advances paid against final liability
- **Deadline:** annual return by 31 May; TAO advances 30 Apr / 31 Jul / 31 Oct

## 3. Local Business Tax (Helyi iparűzési adó, HIPA)
- [ ] File HIPA return with the seat municipality ([CITY])
- [ ] Pay advances and reconcile
- **Deadline:** annual return 31 May; advances 15 Mar / 15 Sep
- Rate: up to 2.0% of net adjusted revenue (municipality-dependent)

## 4. VAT (ÁFA)
- [ ] Confirm VAT status (registered / KATA-style exemption / reverse-charge for EU B2B)
- [ ] File periodic returns (monthly / quarterly per turnover)
- [ ] Confirm NAV Online Számla connection is active for all issued invoices
- [ ] VIES validation current for EU B2B reverse-charge
- **Deadline:** quarterly 20 Apr / 20 Jul / 20 Oct / 20 Jan; annual 25 Feb

## 5. Ultimate Beneficial Owner Register (UBO / tényleges tulajdonos)
- [ ] Verify UBO data is current in the central register
- [ ] Re-confirm annually even if unchanged
- **Deadline:** by 31 December (annual confirmation)

## 6. Managing Director Social Security (Ügyvezető TB)
- [ ] Confirm the managing director's social-security arrangement (employment / agency contract / exemption if insured elsewhere, e.g. via a separate full-time job)
- [ ] Monthly contribution filings ('08) current
- [REVIEW] If the managing director is insured through another full-time employment, confirm the exemption applies for this year.

## 7. Corporate Housekeeping
- [ ] Registered seat (székhely) still valid; seat-service contract not expired
- [ ] Member and director details in the company registry (cégjegyzék) accurate
- [ ] Articles of association (társasági szerződés) reflect current reality
- [ ] Company books / minutes of member decisions up to date
- [ ] Business bank account reconciled

## 8. Data Protection & AI Act (cross-reference)
- [ ] Run `checklists/gdpr-readiness.md`
- [ ] Run `checklists/eu-ai-act-self-assessment.md`
- [ ] Sub-processor list (`clauses/sub-processor-list.md`) reviewed and current

## Usage
- Attach the **standard footer** to every client-facing AI deliverable (report, draft, analysis).
- Use the **deepfake label** for any AI-generated/edited image, voice, or video of a real or realistic person (ElevenLabs voice, Seedance video).
- The **minimal label** is for space-constrained contexts (slides, social posts).
- This is a transparency notice, not a substitute for the contractual AI-accuracy clause (clauses/ai-accuracy-disclaimer.md) in agreements.

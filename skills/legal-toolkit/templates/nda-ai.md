## EN

**NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT**

Between **[PARTY_A]** ([ADDRESS_A]) and **[PARTY_B]** ([ADDRESS_B]), dated [DATE]. This is a [mutual / one-way: [DISCLOSER] discloses to [RECIPIENT]] agreement.

**1. Purpose.** The parties wish to explore/perform [PURPOSE, e.g. a consulting engagement] and may exchange confidential information for that purpose only.

**2. Confidential Information.** Any non-public information disclosed in any form, including business plans, methodologies, prompts, datasets, source code, client lists, pricing, and the existence and content of discussions. Excludes information that is public through no fault of the recipient, already lawfully known, independently developed, or rightfully received from a third party.

**3. Obligations.** The recipient shall: (a) use the information only for the Purpose; (b) protect it with at least reasonable care; (c) disclose it only to personnel/contractors who need to know and are bound by equivalent confidentiality.

**4. Compelled Disclosure.** If legally compelled to disclose, the recipient shall give prompt notice (where lawful) and disclose only what is required.

**5. AI Systems.** The recipient shall not input the disclosing party's Confidential Information into any third-party artificial intelligence service, large language model, or similar tool unless that service operates under a contract that (i) prohibits training on the input and (ii) provides confidentiality at least equivalent to this Agreement. [REVIEW — tighten or relax depending on the sensitivity of the data exchanged.]

**6. Term.** Confidentiality obligations last [3 / 5] years from disclosure (indefinitely for trade secrets while they remain secret).

**7. No Licence.** No intellectual-property rights are granted. Information remains the discloser's property.

**8. Remedies.** Breach may cause irreparable harm; the discloser may seek injunctive relief in addition to damages.

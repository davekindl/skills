**Competition:** Near zero
**Timing:** PERFECT -- just inaugurated, emotional peak

## U6: Hungarian Absurdist Expressions (Bilingual Humor)

**Why untapped:** Hungarian has the most absurd idiomatic expressions in Europe. They translate hilariously. Current "Hungarian humor" merch is generic flags and goulash. The LANGUAGE is untapped comedy gold.
**Audience:** 2M+ Hungarian diaspora, language learners, expat partners, tourists
**Competition:** Zero (bilingual angle)
**Timing:** Evergreen

**U6-A: Language Lesson Series**
```
Bilingual humor t-shirt design, educational poster aesthetic,
header: "HUNGARIAN LESSON #[N]",
large Hungarian text: "[ABSURD EXPRESSION]",
smaller English translation below: "[LITERAL TRANSLATION]",
vintage classroom poster style, 2-color palette,
no background, transparent, isolated design element, print-ready
```

**Expressions to use:**
- "Nem vagyok cukor, nem olvadok el" -- "I'm not sugar, I won't melt" (rain attitude)
- "Apja nem volt uveges" -- "Your dad wasn't a glassmaker" (you're blocking the view)
- "Kutyabol nem lesz szalonna" -- "You can't make bacon out of a dog" (leopard/spots)
- "Annyi esze van, mint egy kilo funek" -- "Has as much sense as a kilo of grass"

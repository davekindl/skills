## N1: Anime / Manga Streetwear

**Market:** $3.3B anime apparel (2024), 10% CAGR. US anime merch projected $1.2B by 2030.
**Competition:** HIGH broad, MEDIUM in sub-niches, LOW in original-art-inspired designs
**Price:** $25-35 sweet spot, premium hoodies $45-55
**Audience:** 16-35, urban, male-dominated but growing female "subtle fandom" segment

### Hot Franchises (2025-2026)
One Piece (surged to 90/100 search index), Demon Slayer, Jujutsu Kaisen, Chainsaw Man, Frieren (breakout), Solo Leveling, Naruto (evergreen)

### Sub-Style Prompts

## N6: AI / Tech Culture (Deep Dive)

**Competition:** LOW-MEDIUM for genuinely clever designs
**Price:** $30-40 (tech workers pay more for quality)
**Audience:** Developers, ML engineers, startup workers, 25-45

**Design rules:** Minimalism. Monospace fonts. Dark shirt designs. Inside jokes = features, not bugs.

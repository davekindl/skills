# Sample Song Concepts — Starter Material

Five generic starter concepts in the format Step 1 expects: title (EN + HU), genre,
emotional angle, key hook. Use one as-is for your first run, then replace this file with
your own researched concepts. Cross out or delete a concept once you have produced it,
and log the finished song in the bible's Song Catalog (section 2).

## 1. First Light / Első fény

- **Genre:** Cinematic hip-hop, orchestral build, ~90 BPM
- **Emotional angle:** The 5 AM empty gym as sacred space — ritual, not grind
- **Key hook:** "The day starts before the sun clocks in"
- **Blueprint hint:** Slow-build orchestral hip-hop; whispered verses, choir-backed final chorus

## 2. Iron Ledger / Vasnapló

- **Genre:** Doom metal, ~72 BPM, down-tuned
- **Emotional angle:** Every rep is a debt paid to your future self — obligation as power
- **Key hook:** "The ledger never lies, the ledger never sleeps"
- **Blueprint hint:** Classic doom structure; riff-led, sparse verses, massive chorus

## 3. Last Set / Utolsó sorozat

- **Genre:** Industrial / EBM, ~128 BPM
- **Emotional angle:** The final set where everything empties — controlled destruction
- **Key hook:** "Leave it all under the bar"
- **Blueprint hint:** Four-on-the-floor stomp; spoken verses escalating to shouted chorus

## 4. Carry the Quiet / Vidd a csendet

- **Genre:** Trip-hop, ~85 BPM
- **Emotional angle:** Recovery day as meditation — strength as stillness, not noise
- **Key hook:** "The muscle grows in the silence between the storms"
- **Blueprint hint:** Downtempo, female or soft male vocal, minimal percussion, warm bass

## 5. Second Wind / Második levegő

- **Genre:** Folk metal stomp, ~100 BPM
- **Emotional angle:** The comeback after failure — falling is data, rising is identity
- **Key hook:** "The floor taught me how tall I am"
- **Blueprint hint:** Chant-driven; group vocals on chorus, verse-to-chorus energy jump
